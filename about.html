Hi, I'm Star Su! I'm a rising junior at Brown University , studying computer science and literary arts. I like to write a lot, whether its code or words in a notebook. I'm interested in the creation of connections, between identities, people, worlds.

Currently, I'm a researcher at the Crowds + Machines Lab at the University of Michigan, exploring how to create more effective team structures.

At school, I'm a teaching assistant for CSCI 0330 Intro to Computer Systems, an experience that never fails to humble and remind me of the magic of computer science. I'm also involved in a number of publications on campus, from VISIONS to The Indy, where I'm grateful to create for a community that strives to address systems of oppression.
